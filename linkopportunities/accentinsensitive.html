Accent-Insensitive Matching (Keyword: seo)

Mode A — Accent-Insensitive: ON

Accented variants present: séó, séo, and decomposed forms using combining marks. Links should keep original glyphs (no normalization in output text).

We discussed séó trends today.

Our toolkit supports séo and seo equally.

Decomposed: séo (e + U+0301).

Another decomposed variant: seó (o + U+0301).

Mode B — Accent-Insensitive: OFF (Strict)

In strict mode, seo matches only the unaccented form. Variants like séó, séo, séo, and seó must not be linked.

Plain seo should match; séó should not.

séo and séo remain plain in strict mode.

seó (o + U+0301) remains plain; only seo matches.
