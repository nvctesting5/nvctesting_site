SEO Basics (Quick Explanation)

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps search engines understand your content and decide when it should appear for a user’s query. Strong SEO usually comes from three areas:

  • Content relevance: Your page answers the searcher’s question better than alternatives.
  • Technical quality: Your site loads fast, works on mobile, and can be crawled easily.
  • Authority: Other sites mention and link to you, showing credibility.

Simple SEO Checklist

  • Write a clear title and meta description that match the page topic.
  • Use one <h1>, then organize content with <h2> and <h3>.
  • Add descriptive alt text to every important image.
  • Link to related pages in your site (internal linking).
  • Compress images so your page loads quickly.
  • Make sure your site works well on mobile.
