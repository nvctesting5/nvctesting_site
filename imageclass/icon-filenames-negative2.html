Filename “Half Match” – Decorative Rule Verification

Rule: Mark Decorative if the filename contains any of: sprite, spritesheet, icon, ico, glyph, chevron, xmark.

Guard Cases (Expected: NOT Decorative, but HALF-MATCH may misclassify)

These filenames include the trigger as part of a larger word (e.g., iconic, icoffee). If your “half match” is pure substring matching, these may be incorrectly marked Decorative.
